Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your products safe for all pets?

A: While our products are designed with pet safety in mind, supervision is always recommended when introducing a new toy or product to your pet. Please check the product description for any specific age or size recommendations.

Q: What materials are your toys made of?

A: We use high-quality, pet-safe materials in all our toys. Specific materials are listed on each product page in the description.

Q: How do I wash or clean my pet clothing/accessories?

A: Care instructions vary depending on the material. Cotton items can be machine washed on a gentle cycle with like colors and tumble dried low. Items with delicate embellishments or faux fur should be hand washed and laid flat to dry. Please refer to the specific care instructions on the product label for details.

Q: Can I change or cancel my order after it's been placed?

A: Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your order. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes once the order has been processed.

Q: What if my order arrives damaged?

A: If your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos of the damaged items. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.
